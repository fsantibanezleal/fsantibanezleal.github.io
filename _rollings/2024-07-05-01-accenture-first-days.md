Started at [Accenture Industry X](https://www.accenture.com/us-en/insights/industry-x-index). The corporate world is a different beast -- more structured, more processes, more acronyms than I can keep track of. But there's something exciting about working at the intersection of technology and strategy for mining companies. The problems are similar to what I've seen before, but the perspective is wider now. Instead of being inside one operation, I'm looking across multiple clients and industries. Different pace, but I think I'll like it here.
