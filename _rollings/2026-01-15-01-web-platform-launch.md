Going Live
======

The web platform for crusher wear management went into its first production deployment. Next.js frontend, FastAPI backend, PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker Swarm, Traefik. A lot of moving pieces but it works. Ansible playbooks handle the provisioning so spinning up a new environment is repeatable. Feeling proud of what a small team can build when everyone owns their piece.
