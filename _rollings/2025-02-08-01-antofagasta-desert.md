Working at Minera Centinela in Antofagasta and the Atacama desert is something you have to see in person. Coming from the forests of southern Chile, this is the opposite end of the country in every sense. Dry, vast, and strangely beautiful. The mine itself is massive -- you feel small standing next to those trucks. The sunsets out here are unreal though. Not a bad office view.
