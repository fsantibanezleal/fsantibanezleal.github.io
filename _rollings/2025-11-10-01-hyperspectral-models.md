Back to Hyperspectral
======

Working on mineral classification models from spectral data again. This time it is not just research -- it is becoming a product. Training clay classifiers for kaolinite, chlorite, and smectite from VNIR/SWIR imagery. The connection between my PhD work on hyperspectral imaging and this consulting project feels full circle. Same physics, same math, but now it has to run reliably in a field deployment.
